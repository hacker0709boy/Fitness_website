About Us

Active members can access Club Fitness as they choose 365 days a year. The Club consists of a main floor area, upstairs training areas, outdoor training area, 2 x unisex showers, 3 x unisex toilets, seperate male/female changing rooms and kitchen facilitys are available for members use. Our staffed hours are found on our home page if you need to talk to a staff member directly. After joining you book an appointment for a tour, general rundown and to get your Club 247 access tag. Should you require any help with starting a basic new program you can download our app then workouts are delivered directly to the app and each exercise has video instructions which makes things easy for you to get started. All of our memberships are ACCESS ONLY, we do however offer personal training as an additional service which is also an additional charge. Simply contact us for more information about personal training.
